Social and Community Development

The Social and Community Development Committee focuses on promoting social interaction between students, faculty, administrators, as well as other schools and organizations around grounds. The committee hosts internal council events, as well as school-wide events. Some examples of past events include:

  • Rotunda Dinner
  • Commerce/Nursing/Engineering Mixer
  • F(date) during E-Week
  • Pie-a-Professor on Pi Day
  • Trivia Nights
  • Lip Sync Battles
